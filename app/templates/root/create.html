How   to Create an Account

Follow these simple steps to create your account:

  1. Step 1: Visit Our Website

    Go to nikonekti.vercel.app/ and click on the "Get Started" button.

  2. Step 2: Fill in your credentials

    Fill in the required fields, including your email address, password, and any other requested information.
    Or you can just continue with google.

  3. Step 3: Verify Your Email

    Check your email inbox for a verification link and click on it to confirm your account.

  4. Step 4: Log In

    Once your email is verified, you can log in to your account and start using our app.
